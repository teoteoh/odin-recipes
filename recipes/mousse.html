The Delectable Passion Fruit Mousse

But what is the Passion Fruit Mousse?

Passion fruit mousse is a delicious dessert sure to win your guests over! When the cremosity of the traditional mousse and the sheer flavourfulness of the passion fruit join their strenghts together, few are able to resist their call. The result is a fresh, unique dessert that will stay in the minds of those who eat it for eternity, an unforgettable dessert that will leave your victims always craving for more.

What you will need:

for the Mousse

  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 3 big passion fruits
  • 1 can of milk cream

for the Topping

  • pulp of 1 passion fruit
  • 1/2 teacup of sugar
  • 1/2 teacup of water

How you will make it:

the Mousse

  1. Cut all the passion fruits in half and transfer the pulps to a blender;
  2. Blend for 2 minutes;
  3. Transfer the juice to a colander and discard the seeds;
  4. Mix the juice, the condensed milk and the milk cream and blend for 3 minutes in high speed;
  5. Pour it on a bowl or individual servings and leave it on the freezer for at least 4 hours.

the Topping

  1. Pour all ingredients for the topping in a pan
  2. Mix it all and take to medium fire
  3. Mix nonstop for 5 minutes or until it becomes smooth
  4. Wait for it to get cold and pour over the mousse
  5. Return them to the fridge and serve after it's cold
